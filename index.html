Our Aim!

To build a community where like-minded people want to reach their study destination.

What we do

We look for enthusiasts who ask questions and learn more about US Universities' application process, clarify others' doubts, speak politely with everyone, and have a clear motive towards their life goals.

Work as a Team!

Our community enables aspiring students to meet other students who have similar interests and goals. Our community also helps each other with test preparations!

Always ready to help

We realize how hard of a task college planning for your future education can be. That is the exact reason for the very existence of this community. We all have been there, being clueless about the entire college process. Tests, internships, competitions, dates, deadlines, essays, the entire process is mentally draining and exhaustive. It would ease a little bit with some help, wouldn't it? We are here to help when you need it. Always!

We aim to help you succeed!

We aim to not only help fellow students through the application process but to make a community full of like-minded and aspiring individuals who are willing to share their experiences and aid others through the college process. We strive to help YOU succeed, and make the arduous task of college preparation easier and memorable.
